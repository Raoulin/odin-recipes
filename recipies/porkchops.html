Pork Chops

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 3 packets of saltine crackers, crushed
  • 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 6 thin cut pork chops

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. In a shallow bowl or pie plate, whisk eggs with garlic and onion powder. Place crackers on a seperate plate. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Dip porkchops into the egg, then press in crackers to coat. Fry in oil for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown. Remove to a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes in oven, turning once. Serve immediately.
