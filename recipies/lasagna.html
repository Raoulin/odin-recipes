Lasagna

Description

This is an easy recipie for lasagna created by Liz Peak, found on allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 10oz lasagna noodles
  • 1 1/2lbs lean ground beef
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1(6oz) can tomato paste
  • 2(6.5oz) cans of tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8-10 min or until aldente; drain.
  3. In a skillet brown ground beef. Using a fork mix cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, parsley salt and papper. In a seperate bowl combine tomato paste and tomato sauce.
  4. In a 9x13 inch baking pan layer noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixture and cottage cheese mixture. Two layers of each will fit. Cover with tin foil and bake fopr 30 minutes. Removove tin foil for last 8 minutes.
