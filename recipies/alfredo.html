Chicken Alfredo

Description

A recipie for Chicken Alfredo by Supertat on allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast cut into chunks
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 packages of thin spaghetti
  • 4 jars of alfredo sauce
  • 2 tablespoons italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped basil
  • cayenne pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink and juices run clear. 7-10 minutes
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil. Cook thin spaghetti in the boiling water until tender yet firm to the bite. 8-10 minutes
  3. Meanwhile, add alfredo sauce to the chicken along with seasoning. Bring to slight boil. Stir in parmesan cheese. Cook mixture until cheese is melted and incorperated into the sauce.
  4. Drain cooked pasta. Scoop into serving bowls and pour sauce over top.
